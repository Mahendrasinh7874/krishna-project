About Us

Our Story

For over 35 years, our Shop has been the go-to destination for those seeking the best quality flour. With decades of experience in the industry, we pride ourselves on offering a wide range of premium flours, carefully sourced and selected to meet the highest standards

Who We Are ?

We are a family-owned business with deep roots in the community, proudly led by Balaram Kumavat. For 35 years, Balaram has been at the helm, ensuring that our shop upholds its reputation for quality and trust.

What We Do ?

At our shop, we specialize in making different types of flour to meet the diverse needs of our customers. Whether it's all-purpose, whole wheat, gluten-free, or specialty flours

Why Choose Us

When it comes to flour, quality matters, and that's exactly what we deliver. We are committed to providing the best quality products that you can trust for all your baking and cooking needs.
